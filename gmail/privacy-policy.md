# Privacy Policy for MailCraft AI
_Last updated: 2025-02-20_

MailCraft AI is a Gmail Add-on that helps users generate safe, professional email replies using AI services (OpenAI or Google Gemini).  
We respect your privacy and ensure strict compliance with Google policies.

---

## 1. Information We Access
MailCraft AI accesses only:
- Sender name  
- Email subject  
- Email body and thread context  
- Draft metadata  
- User-provided idea, tone, language, and reply rules  

Only when needed to generate the user's requested reply.

---

## 2. Information Sent to AI Providers
Sent **only when the user clicks “Generate Reply”**:
- Relevant email text  
- User’s idea  
- Tone and language preference  
- Custom reply rules  

Sent over HTTPS directly to:
- **OpenAI** or  
- **Google Gemini**  

Never sent to the developer.

---

## 3. Storage
We **do not** store:
- Email messages  
- Email metadata  
- Generated responses  
- Personal information  
- Logs  
- Analytics  
- Any content outside Google  

What is stored locally using UserProperties:
- API key  
- Provider selection  
- Custom rules  

These remain inside the user’s Google account.

---

## 4. API Keys
- Not transmitted to the developer  
- Used only to send requests to the chosen AI  
- User can delete them at any time  

---

## 5. Data Sharing
We **never** sell, share, or transfer Gmail data to any third party except the AI provider chosen by the user.

No developer access is possible.

---

## 6. Safety Filters
We use a strict safety filter:
- Blocks harmful or abusive content  
- Prevents insults or harassment  
- Downgrades aggressive tone  
- Ensures replies are polite and professional  

---

## 7. User Rights
Users can:
- Uninstall at any time  
- Revoke permissions  
- Delete saved settings  
- Avoid sending any data by not using “Generate Reply”  

---

## 8. Updates
We may update this policy and will publish changes here.
